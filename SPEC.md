# FC 26 Coins Website — Project Specification

## Overview

A sales-focused, English-language website for selling FC 26 (FIFA) coins, targeting PS4/PS5 players. The site must be optimized for conversion, trust, and ease of use. The designer/developer should think like a sales and marketing expert who deeply understands this audience.

---

## 1. Trust Stack (Above the Fold)

- **Live Ban-Monitor widget/ticker**: Displays real-time reassurance, e.g. "0% ban promise. 100% Secure."
- **Speed Guarantee**: Delivery usually within 2 hours, guaranteed within 24 hours.
- **Trust badges**: Visible above the fold to immediately establish credibility.

---

## 2. The Shop (Core Sales Flow)

### Product
- FC 26 Coins — PlayStation 4 and PlayStation 5.

### Configure Your Order (Step-by-step UI flow)
1. **Platform selection** — Customer picks PS4 or PS5.
2. **Coin amount selection**:
   - Preset buttons (e.g. 250K, 500K, 1M, 2.5M, 5M, 10M).
   - A slider for custom amounts.
   - A live order summary always visible showing the current amount and real-time price.
3. **Account details form** (appears after coin amount is selected):
   - EA Account Email
   - Password
   - 6x Backup Codes (with a "How do I find these?" link that opens a YouTube tutorial video inline — easy to close)
4. **Payment method selection** (appears after form is filled in).
5. **Payment / checkout**.

### Dynamic Pricing
- Coin prices vary by platform and over time.
- Must be easy for the site owner to manually update prices at any time.

---

## 3. Social Proof & Authority

- **Trustpilot integration**: A floating widget showing a 4.8+ star rating.
- **Recent Activity Feed**: Small pop-ups (bottom-left), e.g. "Someone from UK just bought 2.5M Coins".

## 5. Design Guidelines

- **Language**: English only.
- **Tone**: Professional, trustworthy, and sales-driven. The site must clearly signal expertise and safety.
- **Typography**: To be decided — looking for a recommendation that fits the gaming/esports audience.
- **Color scheme / look & feel**: Open for discussion and sparring.
- **Key design principles**:
  - Conversion-optimized layout.
  - Trust signals visible at every step.
  - Clean, modern aesthetic suited to a young, gaming-savvy audience.
  - Mobile-friendly.
